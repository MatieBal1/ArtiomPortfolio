FloodAVille – Project Description

FloodAVille is a 3D survival game that teaches players how to stay safe during a flood. Navigate rising waters, gather essential supplies, and make quick decisions to protect yourself and your community. Learn real-world flood survival strategies through interactive gameplay that turns emergency preparedness into an immersive experience.

FloodAVille – User Comments

FloodAVille received praise from many users for its unique and compelling concept. Players found the idea of combining survival mechanics with educational flood preparedness both creative and impactful. However, as the game was developed as a “University project, time constraints limited the inclusion of all planned mechanics and assets. As a result, FloodAVille remains an open project—one we're excited to continue developing and expanding in the near future.
