Unhinged – Project Description

“Unhinged” is a 3D shooting game created for Game Jam. As you progress, the character constantly mutters "Nothing can go wrong"—a mantra that seems reassuring but only increases in frequency as paranoia sets in. The worse your mental state, the more often you repeat it. Before each kill, you must observe your target. You live their life for a while, mimicking their routine to avoid suspicion. At the end you face the ultimate killer and you have a choice: Kill or Die. Each method has risks. But one thing remains true — you never truly die.
